# USER JOURNEY

**Journey 0: New User Entry**  
_"I just found this app — what is it?"_

A new user arrives at Recipe Box and is greeted by a landing page that introduces the purpose and intent of the application. After reading through the welcome content they are prompted to get started. They are directed to an authentication form where they can toggle between registering and logging in. Since they are new, they fill out the registration form providing their first name, last name, username, email, and password. They confirm their password and upon successful registration are logged in and redirected to the home page for the first time. Returning users who already have an account land on the same authentication form, log in, and are taken directly to the home page.

**Journey 1: Browsing and Discovering Recipes**  
_"I need something to cook tonight"_

The user opens Recipe Box and lands on the home page. From the navigation they head to the Browse Recipes page where they are presented with a search bar and a collection of recipes pulled from the MealDB library. They type in an ingredient or meal name and the results update to reflect their search. They scan through the results, find something that catches their eye, and click on it. They are taken to the full recipe profile page where they can see the meal name, category, cuisine, ingredients, and step by step cooking instructions. Everything they need to cook the meal is right in front of them without ever leaving the app.

**Journey 2: Saving a Recipe to Favorites**  
_"I want to come back to this"_

While viewing a recipe profile the user decides they want to save it for later. They click the Save to Favorites button and the recipe is added to their personal favorites collection stored in the app. They navigate to their Favorites page and can see the recipe sitting alongside any others they have previously saved. If they change their mind about a recipe they can remove it from their favorites and it disappears from their collection immediately.

**Journey 3: Creating an Original Recipe**  
_"I want to record a recipe I just created"_

The user has been cooking and wants to save their own original recipe before they forget it. They navigate to the Create Recipe page where they are presented with a form. They fill in the recipe name, category, ingredients, and cooking instructions then save it. The recipe is added to their My Recipes page where all of their original creations live. If they want to refine the recipe later they can click the edit option, make their changes, and save the updated version. If they no longer want a recipe they can delete it and will be asked to confirm before it is permanently removed.

**Journey 4: Managing a Profile**  
_"I need to update my information"_

The user navigates to their Profile page where they can view their personal information including their name, username, and email. If anything needs to be updated they can edit their details and save the changes. If they decide they no longer want their account they have the option to delete it entirely and will be asked to confirm before the account is permanently removed.

**Journey 5: Contacting Support**  
_"I need help"_

If the user has a question or runs into an issue they can navigate to the Contact Us page where they are provided with ways to reach out for support including email, phone, GitHub, and LinkedIn.

**Journey 6: Error Handling**  
_"Something went wrong"_

If the user encounters an unexpected error at any point in the app they are shown a page that reads "Oops, something went wrong. We have captured this error and will work diligently on fixing it." A button labeled Home gives them a clear path back to the home page so they are never left stranded.

**Journey 7: Logging Out**

When the user is finished they log out from the navigation and are returned to the authentication form, leaving their data safely stored and ready for their next visit.
